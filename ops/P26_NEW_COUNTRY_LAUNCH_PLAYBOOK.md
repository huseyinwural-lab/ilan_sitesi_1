# New Country Launch Playbook (FR Pilot)

## 1. Pre-Launch (Weeks 1-4)
*   **Seed**: Hire 2 freelancers to scrape/input 500 listings in Strasbourg.
*   **Dealer**: Cold call 20 Turkish-speaking real estate agents.
*   **Tech**: Enable `FR` in `Country` table (MC1). Verify `fr-FR` SEO routes.

## 2. Soft Launch (Weeks 5-8)
*   **Traffic**: Turn on Google Ads (Geo: Alsace).
*   **KPI**: 500 Daily Visits.

## 3. Hard Launch (Month 3)
*   **Monetization**: Free for 6 months (Aggressive acquisition).
*   **PR**: Press release in local community media.
