# P25: Reveal Abuse Report

## 1. The "Scraper" Signature
A user who views phone numbers but never calls or messages is likely a scraper or lead farmer.

## 2. Thresholds
*   **Normal User**: 3-5 reveals / day.
*   **Power User**: 10-15 reveals / day.
*   **Bot/Scraper**: > 50 reveals / day OR > 10 reveals in 5 mins.

## 3. Mitigation
*   **Hard Limit**: Max 50 reveals/day per account.
*   **Soft Limit**: After 10 reveals, show ReCaptcha.
*   **Shadowban**: Show fake phone numbers to identified bots.
