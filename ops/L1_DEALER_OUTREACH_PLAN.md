# FAZ-L1: Dealer Outreach Plan

## 1. Value Proposition
"Reach international buyers (Turks in Germany) that other platforms miss."

## 2. Outreach Channels
*   **Direct Email**: Cold outreach to 200 dealers.
    *   Subject: "New marketplace for [City] real estate".
*   **LinkedIn**: Connect with agency owners.
*   **Physical Visits**: Berlin-only pilot.

## 3. Onboarding Process (White-Glove)
1.  **Contact**: Sales rep calls dealer.
2.  **Agreement**: Verbal/Email agreement on Free Trial.
3.  **Setup**: We create the account and import their listings manually.
4.  **Handover**: Send login credentials + "How to use" guide.

## 4. Targets
*   Week 1: 50 Outreach -> 5 Signups.
*   Week 2: 100 Outreach -> 15 Signups.
*   Week 4: 20 Active Dealers posting daily.
