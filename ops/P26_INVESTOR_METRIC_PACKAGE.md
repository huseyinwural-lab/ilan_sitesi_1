# Investor-Ready KPI Package

## 1. Financial
*   **MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)**: Recurring dealer subs.
*   **CMGR (Compounded Monthly Growth Rate)**: Target 15%.
*   **LTV/CAC Ratio**: Target > 3.0.

## 2. Marketplace Health
*   **Liquidity Ratio**: `Transactions / Listings`.
*   **Buyer Retention**: `D30` (Day 30 retention).
*   **NPS (Net Promoter Score)**: Measured via email survey.

## 3. Tech & Product
*   **Velocity**: Deployment frequency.
*   **Uptime**: 99.9%.
