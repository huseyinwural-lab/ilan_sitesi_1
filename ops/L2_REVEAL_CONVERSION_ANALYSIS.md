# L2: Reveal Conversion Analysis Plan

## 1. Metric: Reveal Rate
`Rate = Total Reveals / Total Detail Views`

## 2. Benchmarks
*   **Excellent**: > 5%
*   **Good**: 2% - 5%
*   **Poor**: < 1%

## 3. Drop-off Analysis
Why do users view but not contact?
*   **Trust**: Seller looks shady (No profile photo, no verification).
*   **Description**: Too short, missing details.
*   **Price**: Hidden costs?

## 4. Optimization Actions
1.  **Trust Injection**: Show "Identity Verified" prominently near CTA.
2.  **Urgency**: "2 other people are viewing this listing".
3.  **Friction**: Remove login requirement for Reveal (Soft Gate) - *Requires policy change*.
