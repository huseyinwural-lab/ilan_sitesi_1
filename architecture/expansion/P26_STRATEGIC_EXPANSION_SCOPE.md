# P26: Strategic Expansion Scope Lock

## 1. Objective
Scale the platform from a "Real Estate Niche" to a "General Classifieds Leader" and activate revenue streams.

## 2. In-Scope

### 2.1. Vertical Expansion: Vehicle (Vasıta)
*   **Categories**: Cars, Motorcycles, Commercial Vehicles.
*   **Data Model**: Make/Model/Year/KM/Fuel/Gear.
*   **Search**: Specific filters (Year Range, Mileage).

### 2.2. Monetization Reboot
*   **Free Mode Phase Out**: New users get limited free listings (2).
*   **Paid Plans**: Active for Dealers.

### 2.3. Geographic Expansion
*   **Criteria**: Only expand to FR/CH/AT if DE liquidity is healthy (> 1000 listings).

## 3. Success Metrics
*   **Vehicle Listings**: 500+ in first month.
*   **Revenue**: First €1,000 MRR.
