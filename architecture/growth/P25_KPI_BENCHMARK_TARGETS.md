# P25: KPI Benchmark Targets (First 30 Days)

    *   Recommendation API: < 150ms
*   **Error Rate**: < 0.1% (5xx responses).
*   **ML Fallback Rate**: < 5% (Circuit breaker triggers).

## 2. Growth & Engagement (Business)

    *   Target: 1,000+ (Soft Launch Phase).
    *   Growth: +5% Week-over-Week.
*   **Retention**:

    *   Day-7: > 20%
*   **Engagement**:
    *   Avg Session Duration: > 3 mins.
    *   Pages per Session: > 5.

## 3. Monetization (Revenue)
*   **Conversion Rate (CVR)**:
    *   View -> Contact: > 2.0%
    *   Free -> Premium Listing: > 5.0%
*   **ARPU (Avg Revenue Per User)**:
    *   Target: > $0.50 (Blended).
