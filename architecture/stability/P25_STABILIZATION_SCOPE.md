# FAZ-P25: Stabilization Scope Lock

## 1. Objective
Achieve "Operational Excellence" by stabilizing performance, hardening security, and optimizing conversion funnels before further expansion.

## 2. In-Scope Focus Areas

### 2.1. Performance Engineering
*   **Search API**: Reduce P95 latency from ~800ms to < 500ms.
*   **Listing Detail**: Improve Time-to-Interactive (TTI).
*   **Database**: Optimize query plans for "My Listings" and "Search".

### 2.2. Conversion Optimization (CRO)
*   **CTR**: Improve "Search to Detail" click rate via card design tweaks.
*   **Reveal Rate**: Increase "Show Phone" clicks via trust signals.

### 2.3. Operational Automation
*   **Fraud**: Automate blocking of spam IPs.
*   **Support**: Auto-categorize tickets.

## 3. Out-of-Scope (Feature Freeze)
*   **New Categories**: No Vehicle or Services launch in P25.
*   **New Countries**: Stick to DE/AT/CH/FR/TR.
*   **New Payment Methods**: Stick to Stripe.

## 4. Success Criteria
*   Search P95 < 500ms.
*   Zero P1 Incidents for 14 days.
*   Reveal Conversion Rate > 3%.
